#Volna (волна) — means "wave" — as in trend wave

#Volna is a fashion trend analytics platform that combines Pinterest and Google Trends data to forecast the rise and fall of luxury fashion brand interest, helping buyers, brands, and researchers make data-informed decisions. #The platform aggregates publicly available Pinterest trend data alongside Google Trends search interest data to produce cross-platform investment signals for 21 luxury fashion brands. Using Holt-Winters exponential smoothing and lifecycle classification, the tool forecasts 6-month brand interest trajectories and categorizes each brand as a Buy, Hold, or Wind Down signal for retail buyers and fashion investors. #Pinterest data is used exclusively to access weekly aggregate trend counts for fashion brand search terms in the US market. No user data, personal information, or private board data is accessed or stored at any point. All analysis is performed on anonymized, aggregate trend signals only. #The intended audience includes retail fashion buyers seeking data-driven purchasing decisions, luxury fashion brands monitoring their market positioning, fashion investors tracking brand momentum, academic researchers studying consumer behavior and fashion cycles, and students building expertise in data science applied to the fashion industry. #This application requests access to the Pinterest Trends API only. Data retrieved will be used solely for trend analysis and visualization within the platform and will not be sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.
